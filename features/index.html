App Features: Power at Your Fingertips

Everything you need to organize your family's essential documents, from passports to insurance policies, with privacy built in from day one.

Document Management

Guided Capture

Encrypted Storage

Documents are encrypted on disk using AES-256 standards. Files are unreadable without the decryption keys.

IDs & Driver's Licenses

Front and back capture in a single document. Perfect for driver's licenses, national IDs, and work badges.

Visas & Documents

Specialized A5 / half-page support for visa stamps and stickers. Full A4 support for e-visas, travel insurance policies, and large documents. Supports multi-page files.

Airline Tickets

Store boarding passes and e-tickets with ultra-frequent countdown reminders.

Flexible Import

Import existing PDF files or images from your photo library. Existing scans import seamlessly.

PRO

Export, Backup & Restore

Generate a single PDF for printing. Export your vault as a password-protected encrypted backup. Restore from any backup via Settings → Import Vault.

Smart Capture

Scan Like a Pro

Precision Assistance

On-screen guides help you align your documents precisely. Different templates for different document types:

  • Passport: with center divider line for open spreads
  • ID card: credit card sized frame
  • Visa / Permit: A5 / half-page guide for stamps & stickers
  • Full Page: A4 guide for e-visas, insurance, & tickets

Fine-Tune Every Scan

Fine-tune your captures with built-in editing tools:

  • Rotate: 90° increments
  • Zoom preview: pinch to check clarity
  • Resolution warnings: if image is too small
  • Adjustable crop frame: align with visual guides

Stay Ahead of Deadlines

Family Profiles

Organize by Person

Separate Profiles

Create a profile for each family member. Keep everyone's documents organized and easy to find.

Profile-Scoped Views

View documents and reminders filtered by person. See only what's relevant.

Quick Switching

Switch between profiles with one tap. Perfect for managing the whole family.

PRO

Unlimited Profiles

Free tier includes 1 profile. Pro unlocks unlimited profiles for the whole family.

On-Device OCR

Smart Date Detection

Type Less. Scan Instantly.

Point your camera at a document and the app will attempt to detect expiry dates (assistive only — always verify). Works with MRZ codes on passports and printed dates on visas.

On-device processing: nothing is uploaded
Always requires your confirmation: you decide what to save, not the app
Confidence scoring: know how reliable the detection is
Optional: can be disabled in settings
Manual entry: fallback always available

Feels Like Home

Dark & Light Mode

Adapts to your system settings. Beautiful in both appearances.

Native Gestures

Swipe between document sides. Pinch to zoom up to 5x for detailed inspection.

Quick Look

Native PDF viewing integration. Fast, familiar, and feature-rich.

Privacy & Security

Your Data, Your Device

Your Data Stays Yours

We designed this so you don't have to trust us with your data. We have no servers and no access. It's just you and your device.

Works Offline

No internet required. Works entirely offline.

No Tracking

No analytics. No ads. No hidden SDKs harvesting your data.

Private Notifications

Reminders never reveal document details. Just "A document is expiring soon."

You Control Sharing

Data only leaves when you explicitly choose to share via the system share sheet. Includes sensitive content warnings.

PIN Lock

Protect your vault with a PIN. Available to all users for free.

PRO

Biometric Lock

Unlock with Face ID or Touch ID instead of PIN. Requires PIN to be set up first.

Screen Capture Protection

Document screens are automatically protected from screenshots and screen recordings. Your sensitive information stays on-screen, not in someone's camera roll.

Batch Operations

Manage Multiple Documents at Once

Long-Press Context Menu

Long-press any document card to instantly access quick actions: Export PDF, Share Original, or Delete. No need to open the document first.

PRO

Select Mode

Tap "Select" to pick multiple documents at once. Use the unified Actions menu to batch export, share, or delete your selection.

PRO

Batch Share

Share multiple original document files at once via your device's share sheet (email, messaging, and more). Files are securely decrypted only during sharing.

Safe Delete with Undo

Accidentally deleted a document? No problem. A brief undo window lets you recover it immediately. After that, deletion is permanent to protect your privacy.
