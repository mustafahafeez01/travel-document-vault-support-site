Pricing Policy

Clear, transparent, and customer-first

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Our Pricing Model

No subscriptions. No recurring fees. Just one price.

I built this app because I was tired of subscription fatigue. You pay once, you own it. Here's what that means:

  • • No subscriptions - Pay once, own it
  • • No recurring fees - No monthly or annual charges
  • • No hidden costs - Price you see is the price you pay
  • • No vendor lock-in - Your documents stay on your device, accessible anytime

Free vs Pro

Start free. Upgrade when your family needs it.

Free Tier

Forever Free

Includes:

  • • 1 profile (yourself)
  • • Up to 5 documents
  • • Smart expiry reminders
  • • Document sharing (PDF export)
  • • PIN Lock security
  • • On-device encryption (AES-256)
  • • Offline access

No tricks. The free tier is genuinely free, forever, with full core functionality.

Pro

$9.99 one-time*

Everything in Free, plus:

  • • Unlimited profiles - Add your whole family
  • • Unlimited documents - Store everything
  • • Combined PDF export
  • • Batch backup & restore (ZIP)
  • • Custom reminder timing
  • • Multi-page capture
  • • Biometric Lock (Face ID / Touch ID)

* One-time purchase price applies to current version (v1.x). Future major updates may be offered separately. See Version Policy below.

Version Policy

What your purchase includes, now and forever.

What You Get With Your Purchase

When you purchase Pro, you receive:

  • • Permanent access to all Pro features in the purchased version
  • •
    Free updates within the current major version (v1.x) including:
    • · Bug fixes and security patches
    • · Performance improvements
    • · New features and enhancements
    • · UI/UX improvements
  • • No expiration - Your purchase never expires
  • • Restore on any device - Use your App Store account to restore on multiple devices

Future Major Versions (v2.0, v3.0, etc.)

If we release a major new version (e.g., Travel Document Vault 2.0) with significant architectural changes or substantial new capabilities, it may be offered as a separate purchase within the same app.

How major version upgrades work:

  • • Major versions (v2.0, v3.0) are released as regular app updates through the same App Store listing
  • • When you update, your v1.x Pro features continue to work-you're not forced to upgrade
  • • New v2.0 features are offered as an optional in-app upgrade
  • • You can choose to stay on v1.x Pro or upgrade to v2.0 Pro-entirely your choice

Our commitment to existing customers:

  • • Your v1.x Pro access never expires-use it for as long as you want
  • • v1.x Pro users will receive 50% off upgrade pricing as a thank you for your support (e.g., $4.99 instead of $9.99)
  • • No pressure, no "your version is outdated" nags-just an optional upgrade when you're ready
  • • Major version announcements will be posted on our website and in App Store release notes

Industry Standard: Many premium apps use similar versioning models. This approach allows us to keep the initial price affordable while ensuring long-term sustainability to continue improving the app.

What Will Always Be Free

We will never paywall these core capabilities:

  • • 1 profile with 5 documents (free tier)
  • • Smart expiry reminders
  • • On-device encryption and privacy
  • • Offline access to your documents
  • • PDF export and sharing
  • • PIN Lock security

The free tier will always remain genuinely useful, not a crippled trial. It's designed for individuals or small families who don't need unlimited storage.

Payment Processing

All purchases are processed through:

  • • Apple App Store (iOS, iPadOS, macOS)
  • • Google Play Store (Android) - coming early 2026

We do not:

  • • Process payments directly
  • • Store your payment information
  • • Have access to your credit card or billing details

Your payment information is handled exclusively by Apple or Google according to their security standards.

Refund Policy

Refund requests must be directed to the App Store (Apple or Google) through their standard refund process:

  • • Apple: Request refund at reportaproblem.apple.com
  • • Google: Request refund through Google Play Store app or play.google.com

App store refund policies typically allow:

  • · 14-90 days depending on jurisdiction
  • · Refunds for accidental purchases
  • · Refunds if app doesn't work as described

Note: We (Travel Document Vault) do not control or process refunds. All refund decisions are made by Apple or Google according to their policies.

Price Changes

We reserve the right to adjust pricing for new purchases at any time. Price changes do not affect:

  • • Existing Pro users - you keep what you paid for
  • • Features already purchased - no retroactive price increases
  • • Free tier users - free features remain free

If you purchase Pro at $9.99, that's your price forever (for v1.x). Even if we increase the price to $14.99 for new users tomorrow, your purchase is honored.

Regional Pricing

Pricing may vary by region based on:

  • • Local currency conversion rates (set by Apple/Google)
  • • Regional taxes and fees
  • • App store policies in your country

The price you see in your local App Store is the final price you'll pay (including any applicable taxes).

No Hidden Fees

We will never charge you for:

  • • Downloading or installing the app
  • • Bug fixes or security updates
  • • Using features you already purchased
  • • Accessing your own documents
  • • Syncing between your own devices (via App Store account)
  • • Customer support

There are no in-app purchases beyond the single Pro unlock. No consumables, no coins, no premium currencies, no surprise charges.

Why We Chose This Model

We chose a one-time purchase model because:

  • • Simplicity - No recurring billing to track or worry about
  • • Accessibility - Affordable one-time cost, not ongoing expense
  • • Trust - Your documents stay on your device, accessible regardless of payment status
  • • Alignment - A document vault is something you set up once and use for years

Subscription models create vendor lock-in. If you stop paying, you lose access to your own documents. That doesn't align with our privacy-first philosophy.
