What You're Probably Using Now

These are all reasonable choices. Each one works - up to a point.

Camera Roll / Photos

Works: Always with you.

Falls short: Buried in thousands of photos with no expiry tracking, no organization, and no security beyond your phone lock.

Cloud Storage

Works: Has offline mode and syncs across devices.

Falls short: You store your passport, check the expiry date, switch to a calendar app to set a reminder, then remember which folder had your spouse's documents. It's three apps doing the job of one-and none of them know it's a passport that expires.

Password Managers

Works: Great for passwords.

Falls short: Not designed for document images, expiry tracking, or family organization. A passport photo in a password field is a workaround, not a solution.

Note-Taking Apps

Works: Flexible and familiar.

Falls short: No expiry reminders, no guided capture, and your passport photo sits next to your grocery list with no encryption.

Paper Folders

Works: Reliable at home.

Falls short: Useless at an airport in another country. And paper doesn't send you a reminder six months before expiry.

"I'll Remember"

Works: Sometimes.

Falls short: Until it doesn't. And it usually fails the night before a flight, when it's too late to fix anything.

Quick Answers

The questions we hear most often.

What happens if I lose my phone?
Your documents live on your device, so a regular phone backup (iCloud or Google Backup) includes them. When you restore to a new phone, your vault comes with it. Pro users can also create encrypted ZIP backups anytime.
Does it work without internet?
Completely. The app stores everything on your device and never needs an internet connection. OCR processing, reminders, and document viewing all work offline.
Is my data really safe?
Your documents are encrypted with AES-256 on your device and protected by the Secure Enclave. There are no cloud servers, no accounts, and no way for anyone - including me - to access your data. PIN lock is free; biometric lock (Face ID/Touch ID) is available with Pro.
Is it really a one-time purchase?
Yes. Pro is $9.99 once.* No subscription, no recurring charges, no "your trial is ending" emails. The free tier is genuinely free - 1 profile, 5 documents, all features including OCR and reminders.

* For v1.x. See Pricing Policy.

Can I use it for more than passports?
Yes. It supports passports, visas, IDs, driver's licenses, insurance cards, airline tickets, and any document type you need. Each type has its own guided capture overlay.
