Passport Renewal Guide

How Long Does Passport Renewal Take? (US, UK, Australia, Canada)

Travel Document Vault

February 10, 2026 · 7 min read
General information only. This article is not legal or immigration advice. Passport processing times change frequently and vary by individual circumstance. Always check your country's official passport authority website for current times before applying.

Key Takeaways

  • How long does passport renewal take varies significantly by country: from 3–6 weeks in Australia to 10+ weeks for standard UK or US processing.
  • Expedited and urgent services exist in all four countries but cost extra — and still take days to weeks, not hours.
  • Peak travel seasons (typically Jan–March and June–August) can extend processing times significantly beyond published estimates.
  • The safest rule: begin renewal as soon as your passport drops below 12 months of validity.
  • If travel is booked and your passport is expiring soon, contact your passport authority directly — most have provisions for emergency travel.

You've just booked flights for a trip in five months, and you're reviewing everyone's travel documents. Then you notice it: one passport expires in five months and three weeks. You need to understand how long passport renewal takes — right now — to know if you're still safe to travel.

The honest answer is that processing times vary, are seasonal, and are sometimes longer than official estimates suggest. This guide covers the current reality across four countries in 2026, along with what to do if your timeline is tight.

Important note: Processing times change frequently. The figures below reflect typical timelines in early 2026, but you should always check your country's official passport authority website for current estimates before applying.

How Long Does Passport Renewal Take in the United States?

As of early 2026, the US State Department publishes the following estimates for passport renewal:

Service Type Processing Time Additional Fee
Routine 6–8 weeks None
Expedited 2–3 weeks $60 additional
Urgent (in-person agency appointment) Days to 1 week $60 additional + appointment required

Urgent appointments at regional passport agencies are available when you have documented travel within 72 hours or need a foreign visa within 4 weeks. You need to book online and bring proof of travel.

The US launched a pilot online renewal program (replacing the traditional paper-based DS-82 form for eligible applicants). Eligible applicants are adults renewing a US passport book who were previously issued a passport at age 16 or older. Check travel.state.gov for current eligibility and availability.

The one caveat: published times are averages. During peak travel demand — particularly the post-winter holiday period in January–March and the summer rush — routine processing can extend to 10–12 weeks. If your travel is within three months, apply for expedited service by default.

How Long Does Passport Renewal Take in the United Kingdom?

UK passport renewals are handled by HM Passport Office. Current processing times in 2026:

Service Type Processing Time Notes
Standard (online) ~10 weeks Check GOV.UK for current advice
1-Week Fast Track ~1 week Additional fee; in-person appointment at passport office
Online Digital Renewal Varies Eligible adults only; typically faster
Premium (same-day) Same day Limited availability; appointment required at specific offices

The UK introduced a digital renewal service for eligible adults. This eliminates the need to send your current passport by post and is generally processed faster than the standard postal route. Check GOV.UK to see whether you qualify.

The 10-week standard estimate assumes no complications. If your application requires additional checks — for example, a first adult passport, a name change, or a child's passport — it may take longer. Children's passports cannot use the online digital service and must go through the standard process.

How Long Does Passport Renewal Take in Australia?

Australian passport renewals are processed by the Australian Passport Office. Current estimates for adults applying in-person or through Australia Post:

Service Type Processing Time Notes
Standard 3–6 weeks Apply at Australia Post or a passport office
Priority 2 weeks Additional fee; available for urgent travel
Urgent 3–5 business days Must visit a passport office in person with proof of travel

Australia's standard processing times are generally shorter than the US and UK, but they can stretch during peak periods. The Australian Passport Office recommends applying at least six weeks before international travel under standard processing.

Child passport applications in Australia are more complex and typically take longer than adult renewals, particularly where parental consent requirements apply.

How Long Does Passport Renewal Take in Canada?

Canadian passports are issued by Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC). Timelines in 2026:

Service Type Processing Time Notes
Mail-in (Service Canada) 8–10 weeks Recommended for non-urgent renewals
In-person (Passport Canada office) 10 business days Appointment required at select offices
Urgent (travel within 5 business days) Same day or next day Proof of travel required; in-person only
Express (travel within 45 days) 2–9 business days In-person at a passport office

Canada does not currently offer an online passport renewal option — all applications require either mailing documents or visiting a Passport Canada office in person. This makes Canada's renewal process more logistically demanding, particularly for applicants in rural areas.

Tips to Speed Up Passport Renewal in Any Country

Regardless of which country you're renewing through, a few things consistently delay applications:

  • Incorrect or incomplete forms. A single missing field can send your application back. Read the instructions carefully and double-check before submitting.
  • Non-compliant photos. Passport photo requirements are specific and strict — background colour, head size, expression, glasses. If your photos are rejected, processing stops.
  • Missing supporting documents. Name changes, first applications, or citizenship confirmations require additional paperwork. Have everything in order before you apply.
  • Applying by mail instead of in-person when time is short. Mail is unpredictable. If your timeline is under 10 weeks, go in-person wherever possible.

What to Do If Your Trip Is Imminent

If you have travel booked within the next few weeks and your passport is expiring, act immediately:

  1. Check whether you qualify for an urgent or emergency appointment. All four countries listed above have provisions for this. Contact the passport authority by phone — don't just look at the website.
  2. Gather all documents before calling. Proof of imminent travel (booking confirmations with dates), current passport, photos, and completed forms. You'll typically need to show all of these to get an urgent appointment.
  3. Check your travel insurance. Some policies include cover for additional costs incurred due to urgent passport renewal — though they typically exclude cases where the renewal was foreseeable.
  4. Consider whether the trip can be rescheduled. Airlines and hotels vary in their flexibility, but many will waive change fees for documented passport emergencies.

The most effective long-term solution is to avoid this situation by tracking expiry dates proactively. Frequently Asked Questions

How long does passport renewal take in the US?

In 2026, routine US passport renewal typically takes 6–8 weeks. Expedited processing takes approximately 2–3 weeks. Appointments at a passport agency for urgent travel can be available within days but require proof of imminent travel.

How long does passport renewal take in the UK?

Standard UK passport renewals currently take around 10 weeks. The online digital renewal service is generally faster for eligible adults. A 1-week fast-track service is available for an additional fee, and same-day premium appointments are possible for urgent cases.

How long does passport renewal take in Australia?

Australian passport renewals typically take 3–6 weeks for applications lodged in-person or at Australia Post. Priority processing is available for urgency. Processing times can be longer during peak travel periods.

Can I renew my passport online?

The US launched a pilot online renewal program for eligible adult applicants. The UK offers a digital renewal option for eligible applicants. Australia and Canada require in-person lodgement for most renewals. Eligibility rules vary by country and applicant circumstances.

What should I do if my passport is expiring and I have travel booked?

Apply for renewal immediately. Most countries offer an expedited or urgent service for an additional fee. If travel is within 2 weeks, contact your passport authority directly to ask about emergency appointment availability. Do not wait — processing times can extend unexpectedly.
