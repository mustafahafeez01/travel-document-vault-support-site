## Quick-Reference: Platform Best Practices

**LinkedIn**
- Post body in the first comment (not the caption) is fine, but URL always in first comment
- 5–8 short paragraphs max
- No hashtag dumps — max 3 relevant hashtags
- Best posting time: Mon–Thu, 8–10am or 12–1pm local

**Twitter/X**
- Thread: tweet 1 is the hook (no link), tweet 5 has the link
- Use numbered tweets (1/5, 2/5 etc.)
- Post Tuesday–Thursday, 8–9am or 6–7pm local

**Instagram**
- 7 slides is the sweet spot — enough to deliver value, not so many users swipe off
- Slide 1 must hook in 3 words or less
- Last slide: single clear CTA — URL in bio or typed in caption
- Best posting time: Fri–Sun, 11am–1pm or 6–8pm local
- Save as a Reel cover if possible for extra reach
